About Me


Hi there, I'm Berhane Wheeler, an aspiring startup founder, studying Computer Science and Business Administration at Berea College. I am profoundly passionate about software engineering and game development.

I released one mobile game, Purse Pursuit in May 2024 available on the Playstore and Appstore. I also believe in the power of education and it's ability to transform lives, leading to the launch of my YouTube Channel, Konita Tutorials which I later renamed to Konita. My YouTube channel, Konita, teaches viewers how to program their own apps and develop video games. Additionaly I document my tech projects on this channel, please do subscribe.

I am well-versed in programming concepts and languages. My main programming languages of choice are C# and Python, but I am more than capable of adapting and applying these skills to new programming languages.

My mission is to create world-changing technology solving the problems of today and to entertain people with world-class innovative games.

Aside from the projects I previously mentioned I've worked on freelance software development, I worked as a Computer Science Teaching Assistant. I am a member of the Color Stack Community, and I took part in the Headstarter AI Fellowship.

Moving forward I want to develop more apps and games, and build solutions to real-world problems that are used by a mass-amount of people.
